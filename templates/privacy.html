Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account details — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly experience:

  • To operate your account and display your expense history and summaries.
  • To send essential service communications such as password reset emails.
  • To analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to improve app features.

We will never use your data for advertising, and we will never sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls limited to authorised personnel.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon a verified deletion request, your data will be removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting services) to operate. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media platforms, or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also have the right to object to or restrict certain types of processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information available on our website. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
